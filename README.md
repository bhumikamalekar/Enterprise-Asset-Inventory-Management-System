# Enterprise Asset Procurement & Inventory Management System

## Overview

The Enterprise Asset Procurement & Inventory Management System is a web-based platform designed to streamline procurement, inventory management, finance tracking, and asset allocation within an organization.

The platform centralizes operational workflows, enabling efficient resource management, improved visibility, accountability, and better control over organizational assets and procurement activities.

## Key Features

### Procurement Management

* Supplier Management
* Tender Management
* Quotation Tracking
* Purchase Order Management

### Inventory Management

* Item Management
* Category Management
* Stock Monitoring
* Low Stock Alerts

### Finance Management

* Invoice Tracking
* Payment Monitoring
* Outstanding Due Tracking

### Asset Allocation Management

* Department-wise Asset Allocation
* Asset Movement Tracking
* Issue and Return Tracking

### Request Management

* Department Request Submission
* Approval and Rejection Workflow
* Request Status Tracking

### Dashboard & Monitoring

* Inventory Overview
